Hi, I'm Reuben Shaw

The Oxblood Zebra was created as a fashion brand with an emphasis on storytelling and creativity. Created by Reuben Shaw, a man of many interest that include film, music, international culture and even anime. Reuben wanted a brand that allowed him to tell his own story through clothing but also allow others to do the same. Combining the independent and sometimes stubborn nature of a Zebra and the rich luxurious color of Oxblood red, the Oxblood Zebra was created. Not being boxed in to one category but allowing for your whole story to be told is what we truly embrace here at the Oxblood Zebra. Fashion is Fiction, so what's your story?
